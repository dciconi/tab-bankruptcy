About Puter

Puter is a third-party web platform that bundles pay-as-you-go access to AI models. You sign in once with your Puter account, and Tab Bankruptcy uses it to call Grok, Claude, GPT, or Gemini on your behalf.

Billing: Puter charges your Puter account directly for AI usage — typically a fraction of a cent per cluster. Tab Bankruptcy is free; the developer takes no cut and never sees your billing or usage details.

Manage your usage and top-ups at the Puter dashboard.

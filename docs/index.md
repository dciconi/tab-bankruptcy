# Tab Bankruptcy

Drowning in open tabs? Declare bankruptcy on tab debt.

Tab Bankruptcy is a Chrome extension that clusters your open tabs into thematic groups using the AI provider you choose, then lets you Keep, Save, or Nuke each group with a single click. Get to inbox-zero for your tabs in under a minute.

## How it works

1. **Click the extension icon** to open the triage view.
2. **See the tab count + a Declare Bankruptcy button.** Plus an "estimated focus tax" sublabel that is funnier the more tabs you have.
3. **Click → AI clusters your tabs into named groups.** "React Hook Wizards", "2026 vacation research", "Dad jokes I forgot to close" — that kind of thing.
4. **For each cluster:** ✅ **Keep** (leave open), 📥 **Save & Close** (archive to Chrome's Reading List), or 💥 **Nuke** (close immediately, with a 5-second undo).

## Bring your own AI

**Default: Puter.js** — sign in once with a Puter account, pay-as-you-go (typically a fraction of a cent per cluster). No API keys to manage. The developer of this extension never sees your billing.

**Power users: BYOK** across xAI (Grok), OpenAI (GPT), Anthropic (Claude), and Google (Gemini). Add as many keys as you want and order them as a fallback chain — if your top key is rate-limited, the next is tried automatically.

## Privacy

The developer of this extension does **not** operate any backend, does **not** collect telemetry, and does **not** receive your data. Your tab metadata is sent only to the AI provider you choose. API keys live on your device only — they never sync across profiles. Click **Reset everything** in Settings any time for a full factory wipe.
